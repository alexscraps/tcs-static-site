Talent Acquisition

We Don’t Just Fill Roles. We Find the Right People.

From direct sellers and channel managers to C-suite leaders, sales engineers, and marketing pros. We bring you the right people who align with your values, culture, and mission.

01
Roots

Deep Roots in the TSD Channel

We know the TSD Channel because we live it. We aren’t your typical recruiting firm. Our team has spent decades inside the channel ecosystem, building the relationships and institutional knowledge that allow us to identify talent others simply can’t find.

Channel-Only Focus

We don’t recruit across industries. The channel is all we do, which means our network is deeper and our judgment is sharper.

Relationship-Driven

Most of our best placements come from candidates we’ve known for years, not cold outreach.

02
Method

AI + Human Intelligence

Combining the power of AI and behavioral science with something you can’t automate: real human connection. We use data to surface candidates and human judgment to evaluate them. The result is faster placements with higher retention rates.

Behavioral Assessments

Understanding how candidates think, communicate, and lead before they ever meet your team.

AI-Assisted Sourcing

Leveraging technology to identify passive candidates who aren’t actively looking but are open to the right opportunity.

Reference Intelligence

Going beyond standard references to build a complete picture of how a candidate performs under pressure.

Retention Tracking

We follow up at 30, 90, and 180 days to ensure placements are succeeding and thriving.

03
Culture

Culture First

Hiring isn’t just about resumes. It’s about culture, alignment, and potential. The best channel professionals aren’t always the ones with the longest list of logos. We look for people who will grow with your organization and represent your brand with integrity.

Values Alignment

Mapping candidate values to your company’s mission before the first interview.

Long-Term Fit

Prioritizing candidates who want to build something, not just collect a paycheck.
