Advisory Services

Strategy that goes beyond the product.

The Channel Standard brings the same intelligence that powers Channel Companion to your business directly. Whether you need to redesign your channel program, sharpen your go-to-market, or understand where your supplier relationships are leaving money on the table, we work alongside your team to build something that lasts.

What we do

Advisory built on channel intelligence, not guesswork.

Every engagement is grounded in data and direct channel experience. We do not sell frameworks. We solve specific problems.

Channel Program Design

Build or rebuild your channel program from first principles. We assess your current structure, identify where incentives misalign, and design a program that recruits the right partners and retains them for the right reasons.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Sharpen how you position, price, and sell through the channel. We work with suppliers and distributors to align GTM motions with how advisors actually make decisions today, not how they did five years ago.

Partner Intelligence

Understand your partner ecosystem with the same rigor you apply to your product. We map advisor behavior, identify high-value segments, and surface the patterns that separate top-performing channel relationships from average ones.

Organizational Design

Structure your channel team to match your growth stage. From hiring profiles to compensation models to reporting lines, we help you build the internal capability to execute a channel strategy, not just describe one.
